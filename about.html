Our History

Pegada paints is Manufactured by Gibstar Engineering Company Limited. It was established in the year 2018 conducting its main operations in the Industrial area Nakuru, Kenya.

Being a progressive paint manufacturer, Pegada has entered the market with a positive reputation of manufacturing paint of high quality standard.

The company strives to develop its products through innovation and creativity to manufacture an extensive range of products designed to meet decorative needs of the market.
