Pegada Emulsion

Pegada Emulsion is a high quality water-borne paint based on acrylic copolymer binder developed to fulfil the rising demand on quality emulsion material required for application purposes in aggressive environmental conditions in the architectural and building industry.

  • Preparation

    • All surfaces must be thoroughly , dry clean and free from rust, oil, grease or dirt. Polish the new surfaces and prime with an appropriate primer before application of Pegada Emulsion.

  • Application

    • Apply by brush, roller.

  • Coverage

    • 10 to 12 Square meters per liter depending on surface porosity.

  • Drying Time

    • 4 to 6 hours in normal drying conditions. Recoat able in 16 hours.

  • Thinning

    • Thin with clean water- avoid over thinning.

  • Equipment Cleaner

    • Clean equipment with clean water immediately after use.

  • Packing Sizes

    • 1 litre, 4 litre and 20 litre.

  • Storage

    • Store in a cool, dry place and keep away from heat sources.

Pegada Silk Vinyl Emulsion

Pegada Silk Vinyl Emulsion is a premium quality pure acrylic based paint. It dries to give a mild sheen gloss level. It is recommended for domestic use as well as hospitals and food processing plants. It can be applied on all types of plastered surfaces, concrete, building boards, bricks, stonework as well as timber.

  • Preparation

    • New Surfaces: All surfaces must be clean and in good condition. Previously painted surfaces must be in clean and in good condition. Loosely bound and flaking paint must be completely removed. Old enamel paint must be sanded to provide adhesion.

  • Application

    • Apply by brush, roller or spray. For best result apply a minimum two coats at 3 to 4 hours intervals.

  • Coverage

    • 12 to 15 meters squared per litre depending on porosity of the surface.

  • Drying Time

    • Dries in about 4 hours in normal weather conditions.

  • Packing Sizes

    • 1 litre, 4 litre and 20 litre

  • Storage

    • Store in cool, dry and well ventilated area.

Pegada Gloss Enamel

Pegada Gloss is an Alkyd pigmented solvent based enamel. It’s a high quality interior/exterior enamel for surfaces where a glossy durable finis is required. It comes in a wide range of of variety of delightful shades and provides an excellent washable finish.

  • Preparation

    • All surfaces must be thoroughly , dry clean and free from rust, oil, grease or dirt. Polish the new surfaces and prime with an appropriate primer before application of Pegada Emulsion.

  • Application

    • Apply by brush, roller.

  • Coverage

    • 10 to 12 Square meters per liter depending on surface porosity.

  • Drying Time

    • Touch dry in 4hours in normal drying conditions. Surface dry in 12 approximately 12 hours in good weather conditions.

  • Thinning

    • Thin with thinner.

  • Equipment Cleaner

    • Clean equipment with clean water immediately after use.

  • Packing Sizes

    • 1 litre, 4 litre and 20 litre.

  • Storage

    • Store in a cool, dry place and keep away from heat sources.
